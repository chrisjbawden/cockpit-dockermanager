Fast

Start exporting logs from your systems or solutions in as quickly as it takes you to run a script. Its purpose is to facilitate rapid development.

Caching

Utilising logstashs built in mechanism for caching and delivery failure detections and resending, you automatically adopt benefitial controls.

Micro

Plumb this up with your other systems to build something bigger or fill a gap. It also means you can deploy as many of these as your project needs.
